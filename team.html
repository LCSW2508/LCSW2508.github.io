Meet Our Team

Who We Are

At Pristine VacuMatic Solutions, we are a close-knit team of professionals passionate about making homes cleaner, quieter, and healthier. Our experienced staff brings innovation, service, and expertise to every project.

Jane leads the company with over 20 years of experience in engineering and clean-tech innovation. She’s passionate about building sustainable, user-friendly products.

Alex develops creative campaigns to highlight the power and convenience of our systems, connecting with homeowners across the country.

Maria works directly with clients to find the perfect vacuum solution, offering exceptional service and technical insight.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a small group of engineers, Pristine VacuMatic Solutions began in a garage with a big dream: to build the quietest, cleanest central vacuum systems in the industry. Today, we’re a nationwide company with a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Did You Know?

Our systems can remove up to 99.97% of indoor air allergens, creating a healthier home for your family.
